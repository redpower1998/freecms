# 简历管理 #
<p>
<blockquote><span>从FreeCMS 1.5开始支持</span></p>
<p>
<span>管理当前管理站点的</span><span>简历</span><span>数据。</span></p>
<p>
<blockquote></p>
<p>
</blockquote><span>1. </span><span>回复简历</span></p>
<p>
<a href='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075607_JPMl_916014.png'><img src='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075607_JPMl_916014.png' alt='' /></a></p>
<p>
<span>选择需要回复的</span><span>简历</span><span>，然后点击"回复"。</span></p>
<p>
<span>注意：同时只能回复一个简历。</span></p>
<p>
<a href='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075627_XDGN_916014.png'><img src='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075627_XDGN_916014.png' alt='' /></a></p>
<p>
<span>输入回复内容后，点击"回复"按钮即可。</span></p>
<p>
<span>2. </span><span>删除简历</span></p>
<p>
<span>选择需要删除的简历，然后点击"删除"按钮。</span></p>
<p>
<span>提示:同时可以删除多个简历。</span></p>
<p>
<a href='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075648_sMdG_916014.png'><img src='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075648_sMdG_916014.png' alt='' /></a></p>
<p>
<a href='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075703_yeQk_916014.png'><img src='http://static.oschina.net/uploads/space/2013/1028/075703_yeQk_916014.png' alt='' /></a></p>
<p>
<span>为了防止误操作，系统会提示用户是否删除，点击"确定"完成删除操作。</span></p>
